MinToDo

Minimalistic ToDo
Android

See the Pen Pomodoro Clock by Haris Siddiqui (@haris-siddiqui) on CodePen.

MinToDo is a material themed minimalistic ToDo app aimed at giving you only what you need to get the job done. Simple in design and function. Quickly add a ToDo item, choose to add a reminder or not, and save it. When the day and time come for the task to be completed, the app will remind you with a notification. Once the task is complete, simply swipe it away. No longer is making a ToDo list a ToDo item, get it done minimalistically with MinToDo.

This project uses Jave, HTML, CSS, and NoSQL.

Scale

Financial Independence Through Social Obligation
React Native

Scale is a budgeting app that introduces a sense of competitiveness among users. You and your friends are ranked by the points you earn for actively using the app. The more you points you have, the more financially responsible you are. Since your friends can also see your Scale score, you have an incentive to gain more points so you're not at the bottom of the stack.

This project uses React Native as the frontend, and Firebase on the backend to co-develop iOS and Android versions.
